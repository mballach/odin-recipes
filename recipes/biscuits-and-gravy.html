Biscuits and Gravy

Description

Biscuits and gravy, a southern delight.

Ingredients

Biscuits

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup cold milk

Gravy

  • 1 pound pork sausage
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Make biscuits.
  2. Make gravy.
  3. Combine.
