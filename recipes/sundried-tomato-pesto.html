Sundried Tomato Pesto Pasta

Description

Pasta served with pesto made from jarred sun-dried tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta
  • 8 oz jar sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in large pot of salted water until tender, stirring occasionally. Drain, reserving 1 cup of cooking liquid.
  2. Blend remaining ingredients until tomatoes are finely chopped.
  3. Add pasta to pesto and toss to coat, adding reserved cooking liquid to moisten.
